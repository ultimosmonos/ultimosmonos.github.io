Cookies

Últimos monos uses cookies, just like any other site nowadays. It does so it can use services from third parties like Goolge or Disqus. That way, we can analyze the web traffic or add features like post comments.

It all started with the European cookie law and, as I’m in Spain, with the modification of the following article Ley 34/2002, de 11 de julio, de servicios de la sociedad de la información y de comercio electrónico in which the following obligations are imposed to the web sites in order to protect the user’s privacy.

  • The user must be informed about the use of cookies.
  • The user must consent the use of cookies on the site.

That’s why you are reading these lines. The cookies shall be dimmed to have been accepted when you start browsing the site, but that annoying message will not disappear until you click the accept button. Once you accept, the message will disappear for an entire year ( I will use a cookie to remember that ).

Cookies details

This site use the following cookies from third party providers:

  • Google Analytics: Stores cookies in order to provide statistics about web traffic and provide information about the number of visits.
  • Disqus: Uses the cookies detailed here in order to allow the integration and management of the post comments in this site.

Deactivating or deleting cookies

You can exercise your right to deactivate or delete the cookies from this website anytime. In order to do that you must access your browser settings, where you can search for, delete and/or block the cookies from this site.
